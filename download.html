Download Instructions 🌸

Thank you for bringing a piece of Paperoremate into your world. Here’s how to download your printable:

1. Click the download link provided after purchase or in your email.

2. Save the file to your device (we recommend a cozy folder called “Paperoremate Goodies”).

3. Open the file using your preferred viewer or design software.

4. For best results, print on waterproof sticker paper or high-quality matte sheets.
